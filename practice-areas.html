Practice Areas

Medical Malpractice Defense

Buckley, Theroux, Kline & Cooley LLC focuses on medical malpractice litigation. Our emphasis is to provide the best defense possible to doctors, nurses, other medical practitioners, medical practices and hospitals in all areas of medicine throughout the New Jersey/Pennsylvania region. All of our attorneys are experienced in complex medical malpractice litigation. The support staff is proficient in handling large files and accompanying sensitive medical records.

Personal Injury

Buckley, Theroux, Kline & Cooley LLC represents clients injured in motor vehicle accidents, slip and falls, and product liability suits. We are aggressive and knowledgeable in fighting for the best result for our clients. Our work in the medical malpractice arena makes us uniquely knowledgeable and allows us to be more effective in arguing our clients' cause. The personal injury matters we handle on behalf of plaintiffs are typically taken on a contingency basis, meaning there is no cost to our clients unless we secure a judgment, verdict or settlement on their behalf.

Municipal Court

Buckley, Theroux, Kline & Cooley LLC represents clients in various municipal courts throughout New Jersey. We are experienced in handling all traffic violation appearances.
