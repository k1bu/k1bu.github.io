Buckley, Theroux, Kline & Cooley LLC is a litigation firm specializing in medical malpractice defense for healthcare professionals and institutions throughout New Jersey and Pennsylvania. Our location in Princeton, NJ allows for convenient representation of clients throughout the state and nearby Philadelphia and Bucks County areas. We have attorneys licensed to practice in both New Jersey and Pennsylvania to best serve our clients. Our firm is experienced in pretrial, trial, and appellate litigation and is able to work at all levels to further the needs of our clients.

Our experienced Partners and dedicated staff guarantees that each client we represent receives the highest level of individual service and attention.
